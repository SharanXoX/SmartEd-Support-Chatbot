## Password resets

Users can reset passwords from the login screen:

1. Click **Forgot password**
2. Enter the email associated with the account
3. Open the reset link within **15 minutes**
4. Choose a **strong password** (12+ characters recommended)

If you do not receive an email within a few minutes, check spam folders or confirm you’re using the correct email address.

## Payments troubleshooting

If a payment fails:

1. Confirm your card supports online purchases and isn’t expired.
2. Retry using another browser or disable aggressive ad-blockers temporarily.
3. If funds were deducted but access didn’t update, contact billing support with your receipt reference.

Never email full card numbers.
